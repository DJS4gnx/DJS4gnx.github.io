Welcome to my site!

I'm Haley, and this is my website. I am an 18 year old student from Canada, and I use this site mainly as a way to update people on what I'm doing, so try not to expect much in terms of graphic design or portfolio work. This isn't a demonstration of my skill, after all, I made this for fun. This domain does not connect you to game servers I own, please send a private message about that instead. I currently use SpaceHey for blogs but may set up a little blog system here at some point. Like what I do? Feel free to bookmark!

I do some archival stuff as I do data hoard, and I am mostly playing games now, but my main thing is my music project R1TALIN, where I post electronic music, made with some Chiptune inspirations. I have also previously done ROBLOX modding stuff, although I'm not active in it anymore. I don't really have a public status page to catch up on anything, so either you should see my SpaceHey bulletins/blogs for that, or you should just reach out to me privately. I'll look into a on-site blog system soon, like RSS. I am currently not available to do any work for anybody at the moment. I'll let you know if that changes.

I do not have any way to purchase my music at the moment. If you would like to receive downloads for new music, please ask me privately, bandcamp free downloads are costly!

This site probably does not look too great, and I already know that. I will be looking into trying to make changes soon that should hopefully minimize the ugliness.

If you need to contact me, the easiest way to reach out is through Discord. My username is driftzoner and friend requests should be on. I am also very active on SpaceHey if you do not like Discord.
I curently do not have a public email and have no plans to get one any time soon, but I will look into it. Send your questions your way, I'll answer as soon as I can!
