Right... I've kept my secrets way too long. Let me introduce myself

Hello, I'm DJSng, a canadian based self-taught musician, ROBLOX rendering artist, passionate modder, blogger and writer. I was also the original director and owner of Enraged Novetus Game Destroyer, a 2020-2021 Old ROBLOX based game ranting skit about a kid playing ROBLOX as if it was really the 2010s.

I have gone by many names over the years, including Zandy, Luma, and Kameron. However, I would prefer you just simply call me Dj.

I started this website back around April 2024 with Sadgurl's template generator, though only briefly. I picked the idea of a site back up around September and slowly built my way back to a cool site! I'm still not quite sure what I want out of it, originally having started it to replace the many Rentry.org and Carrd.co pages I made throughout 2020 - 2024.

Since 2025, I've been trying to outdo myself, by listing a lot of resources I like to rely on, as well as some more stuff focused on what I do. Since then, the site evolved into this. A very slim page that gets right to the point. Just how I like my stuff!
