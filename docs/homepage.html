Hi, welcome to the tavern! Please stay, no matter if you came for a drink, to hear a story, or even to reread every single thing I've written about! The tables are over here, please stay a while as we tell you some stories.

This is my personal blog, I mainly use it to document cool things I remember about, such as topics I like to ramble about, some of my internet journies through things like Roleplay, making this website, and even some rants about stuff that went wrong. There's a mix of everything from angry rants to happy retrospectives. There's something here for everybody, and I just write to be passionate about my interests. This blog is alt text heavy, hover over images, links, and a whole bunch of other stuff for some extra humor and content! I like to add context to everything, and adding alt text helps. This decision was inspired by xkcd doing it with their comics.

Some technical details include that I run everything through the Zonelets blog engine. This page will mirror with my SpaceHey blog as much as possible for parity, especially because I mainly use this page as an archive, while posting to SpaceHey right after it arrives here to get a little bit of interest.

If you have an RSS reader, you can copy this link to add it to your reader! It currently does not have time accuracy, but I'm working on it. I currently use kalechips' Zonelets style.css theme, but I'll look into some more options later. Thank you to everybody who's Zonelets projects I've added to my blog!
